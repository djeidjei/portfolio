Macy's mStyle Lab

Journee

Creative Director

Macy’s wants you to be your best digital self with mstylelab, a new place to connect, shop and explore fashion’s future in an immersive space. Embarking on a new journey to intertwine 160 years of retail heritage with the growing immersive web, Macy’s introduces mstylelab—an immersive platform built to reward loyal Macy’s customers. While spotlighting their fresh new collection On 34th, Mstylelab invites the public into a virtual version of New York with a surreal twist. Featuring in-experience shopping through iframe integration, personalized digital fabrics and customizable avatars powered by ReadyPlayerMe, Macy’s new experience showcases the future of fashion.
